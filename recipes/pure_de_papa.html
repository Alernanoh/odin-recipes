Pure de papa

The pure de papa is the normal mashed potatoes but this site is centered on latin recipes :D


The pure de papas is a normal mashed potatoes recipee but with some latino twist on it


Ingredients

  • potatoes
  • Butter
  • milk
  • salt
  • Cheese
  • Pepper
  • milk cream

  1. Peel and cook the potatoes
  2. Once they're cooked you can mash them
  3. Once they're mashed add some butter and mix
  4. Now add some milk to it so it gets softer
  5. Add the salt and pepper
  6. Now for a little extra flavour and texture add some milk cream
  7. and that's it now you have your mashed potatoes
